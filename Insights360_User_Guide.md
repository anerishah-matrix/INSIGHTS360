# Insights360 BI Dashboard - User Guide

Welcome to **Insights360**, your premium business intelligence platform for deep sales analytics and product performance tracking. This guide will help you navigate and master the features of the dashboard.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Secure Access
- **Authentication**: Insights360 requires a secure login. If you don't have an account, use the **Sign Up** page to register with your corporate email.
- **Persistent Preferences**: Your filter selections (selected products, years, metrics) are automatically saved to your profile and synced across devices.

### 1.2 Initializing Data (Cloud Sync)
Insights360 connects directly to your Google Drive to process Excel data in real-time.
1. **Gather Folder ID**: Copy the ID of the Google Drive folder containing your sales data (the long string of characters in the folder's URL).
2. **Share Permissions (Critical)**: For the system to read your files, you **must share** your Google Drive folder with the following service account:
   `bi-dashboard-reader@bi-dashboard-drive.iam.gserviceaccount.com` (Set as "Viewer").
3. **Paste & Sync**: Paste the Folder ID into the top-bar input and click **Sync Now**.

## 🛠 2. Interface Overview

### 2.1 The Unified Header (Top Bar)
- **Sidebar Toggle**: Click the ☰ icon to collapse the sidebar for a "Full Screen" analysis view.
- **Sync Widget**: Manage your cloud folder connection.
- **Metric Selector**: Toggle between **Value** (Revenue in INR) and **Qty** (Quantity sold).
- **Navigation Dropdown**: Seamlessly switch between the four main analysis modules.
- **Yearly Filter**: Quickly switch the entire dashboard between different Financial Years.
- **User Profile**: Access your profile details or sign out.

### 2.2 The Interactive Sidebar
- **Searchable Filters**: Use the **Products** and **VADs** (Distributors) sections to narrow down your data. Use the search bar for quick selection.
- **View-specific Controls**: Depending on which page you are on, the sidebar provides sub-navigation (e.g., "Time-based" vs "Model-wise" views).

## 📊 3. Analysis Modules

### 3.1 Main Dashboard
- **KPI Cards**: Instantly view Gross Revenue, Transaction Count, Portfolio Size, and Average Deal Size.
- **Performance Trends**: A bar chart showing your sales trajectory. 
- **Drill-down**: Click on any **Year Bar** to see its months, and click a **Month Bar** to see daily performance.
- **Product Donut**: A visual breakdown of your top-performing models.

### 3.2 Regional Analysis
- **Geographic Coverage**: Analyze sales distribution across different zones.
- **Zone Drill-down**: Clicking a Zone bar will switch you to a time-based view specifically for that region.

### 3.3 VAD Analysis (Distributor Insights)
- **Time/Zone/Product Views**: Analyze your distributors' behavior over time, by their location, or by what they buy.
- **Distributor Table**: A ranked list of your top 50 distributors with absolute values and percentage contributions.

### 3.4 Product Movement
- **Target vs Achieved**: Specifically designed for "Product Movement" Excel files.
- **Gap Analysis**: Visualizes how close your products are to their targets across different months.

## 💡 4. Pro Tips for Power Users

- **Save as Image**: Every chart has a download icon 📥 in its top-right corner. Use this to save high-resolution charts for your reports or presentations.
- **Reset Drills**: If you've drilled down deep into a specific month or region, look for the red **Reset** button in the chart header to return to the top level.
- **Comparative Analytics**: In the main Dashboard, select the "Comparative" view in the sidebar to see multiple years' performance graphed on a single line chart for easier year-over-year analysis.
- **Status Indicator**: Check the Status in the top-right; "Connected" (Green) means your data is successfully loaded from the cloud.
